My name is Roxanne Dimaano. I am currently a first year political science major with an emphasis in international affairs at University of California, Riverside. I hope to one day go to law school, pass the bar, and then go into the United Nations to serve as a diplomat. So far in college, I have joined the Philipino Student Organization and will be planning to join Model UN and Women in Law. I am passionate about working in international law.

Not only I want to apply my lessons through my studies but with my career path.I hope to help around the world some day with these lessons and experiences behind me and use them throughout my time in the United Nations in the future.
